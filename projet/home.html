CoviDoNATION Advantages

Transparency

Start donating in less than 10 minutes. No money involved and products will be delivered directly by donatekart, thus complete transparency. When donations from your supporters are transparent, new and returning supporters can see the credibility of your cause to further encourage new donations. Since there is no involvement of any third party, it reduces the risk of fraud

Need Based Donation

Just tell us the products that are in need to your organisation we will make sure that you get them all and you just focus on the work you do. Another advantage of having an online donation page is the ability for new supporters to learn about your cause. In fact, Social Media posts are one of the best ways to build brand awareness when your supporters share with their group of friends.

Free Platform

CoviDoNATION is a Completely free platform for organisations or any individual to run any number of campaigns whereas crowdfunding platforms charges more than 10% of raised money as fees. CoviDoNation is very beneficiery With 100% of donations going towards grants and programs and to the people who need it the most. The only thing needed for being a donor is good intentions.
